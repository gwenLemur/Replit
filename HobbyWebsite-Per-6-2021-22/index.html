Spider Plants

Spider plants are some of the most resiliant and forgiving house plants out there.

Spider plants are non-toxic to pets and can live in any container.

They will fill up all the available space and can even survive in pure water if given proper nutrients.

They can thrive almost anyhere and are some of the best house plants for purifying the air.
